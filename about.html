About Us

We’re a group of local Christians who love our community and love dialogue. We think good ideas are worth talking about and good questions are worth asking because God gave us minds that can appreciate both.

We’re on campus because we want to talk to truth-seekers like us who want to love our communities well by thinking hard. We want to ask the tough questions and hear your answers.

Questions about science, sin, faith, atheism, religion, gender, politics, sexuality, and anything else you may want to talk about.
